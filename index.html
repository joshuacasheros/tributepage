Albert Einstein

One of the most influential scientists.

Timeline of Dr. Albert Einsteins Life

  1. 1879

    Born in Ulm, Germany

  2. 1880

    Father founds Elektrotechnische Fabrik J. Einstein & Cie

  3. 1884

    Is gifted a compass by his farther sparking his lifelong fascination with electromagnetism

  4. 1896

    Renounces his german citizenship

  5. 1901

    Publishes his first paper "Folgerungen aus den Capillaritätserscheinungen"

  6. 1905

    Publishes four groundbreaking papers known as the Annus Mirabilis papers that changed the way people understood space, time, mass, and energy

  7. 1911

    Publishes an article "On the Influence of Gravitation on the Propagation of Light" which further expanded on an article published in 1907, the existence of this article made it so the theoretical prediction of general relativity could be tested experimentally for the first time

  8. 1917

    Discovers that general field equations predicted a universe that was dynamic, either contracting or expanding. He introduced a new term, the cosmological constant, this then allowed the theory to predict Einsten's static universe

  9. 1922

    receives Nobel Prize in Physics

  10. 1931

    Abandons his static universe theory, and proposed two new models of the universe. These were known as the "Friedmann–Einstein universe" of 1931, and "Einstein–de Sitter universe" of 1932

  11. 1939

    Sends a letter to President Roosevelt warning him about the potential German Nuclear weapons

  12. 1940

    Is granted US citizenship

  13. 1948

    Is surgically operated on by Rudolph Nissen, he surgically reinforces Einsteins abdominal aortic aneurysm

  14. 1955

    Dies of heart failure at Princeton hospital, caused by internal bleeding by the rupture of his surgically reinforced aorta
